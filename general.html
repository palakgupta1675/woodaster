WHAT ARE GENERAL TREATMENT ?

Are you tired of cockroaches, ants, flies, or mosquitoes in your home/office? We provide professional General Pest Control Treatment with a 1-year annual package (4 services every 3 months) — so you can enjoy a pest-free environment all year round.

General treatment in pest control refers to the professional methods used to eliminate common household pests like ants, cockroaches, and other insects. It usually involves inspection, use of safe insecticides, baits, or sprays, and preventive measures to stop re-infestation. This treatment ensures a clean, hygienic, and pest-free environment.

SIGNS OF GENERAL INSECTS INFESTATION

→ Cockroach: Infestation is indicated by sightings of cockroaches, droppings, egg cases, and a musty odor in kitchens or dark corners.
→ Ants: Infestation is shown by visible trails, nests near food sources, and scattered crumbs or food being carried away.
→ Spider:Frequent sightings of spiders indoors, along with webs and egg sacs in corners, ceilings, and hidden areas.
→ Lizard: Regular sightings of lizards on walls or near lights, often attracted by insects, with droppings found in corners or behind furniture.
→ Fly: Large numbers of flies seen around food, garbage, or drains, often accompanied by larvae, unpleasant odor, and fly spots on walls or surfaces.

    WHAT ARE ANTI TERMITE RETICULATION PIPING SYSTEM ?

    Once termites enter, they damage wood, furniture, and even walls from the inside. Our Reticulation Piping System provides a long-term solution with piping installed only along wall borders, creating a continuous chemical barrier around your home.

    An Anti-Termite Reticulation Piping System is a network of special perforated pipes laid under or around a building’s foundation/flooring that lets you inject termiticide periodically; this creates a chemical barrier in the soil to prevent termites from entering the structure.

    HOW IT WORKS

    → HDPE pipes are installed along wall borders of rooms.
    → Anti-termite chemical is injected through these concealed pipes, reaching soil-wall junctions.
    → Re-injection can be done every 2–3 years without re-drilling.
    → Creates a protective barrier that blocks termites permanently.

    TIPS FOR ANTI-TERMITE RETICULATION PIPING SYSTEM

    ✓ Site Inspection: assess wall borders & layout for pipin.
    ✓ Piping Installation: install concealed/visible HDPE piping along wall borders.
    ✓ Injection Points: provided at regular intervals for easy chemical recharge.
    ✓ Chemical Injection: high-pressure anti-termite chemical released into soil junctions.
    ✓ Sealing: cover and secure all charging points.
    ✓ Recharging Facility: future re-treatment possible directly from charging points.

    BENEFITS OF ANTI TERMITE RETICULATION PIPING SYSTEM

    ✓ Ensures even, consistent chemical barrier under floors without drilling or excavation.
    ✓ Allows for easy, periodic refilling through junction boxes outside, reducing disturbance.
    ✓ Minimises odor, mess, and damage to flooring or interiors.
    ✓ Cost-effective over long term due to lower maintenance and repair costs compared to traditional methods.
    ✓ Provides long-lasting protection against future termite infestations.
    ✓ Safe and eco-friendly method with minimal chemical exposure indoors.
    ✓ Increases property value by ensuring a termite-free structure.

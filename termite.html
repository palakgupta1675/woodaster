WHAT ARE TERMITE ?

Do you see white powder, hollow wooden furniture, or mud tubes on walls and doors? Termites silently damage furniture, doors, and even building structures. We provide professional termite control treatment — eliminating termites from the root.

Termites are small, social insects known for feeding on cellulose ‒ that is, the wood and plant-based materials in and around buildings. They live in colonies and are often referred to as “white ants,” though they are not ants and belong to their own order (Isoptera).

SIGNS OF TERMITE INFESTATION

→ Hollow-sounding wood.
→ White powder or dust near walls/furniture.
→ Mud tubes on walls or wooden furniture.
→ Faint sounds from inside wood.
→ Swarmers (winged termites) seen near windows or doors.
→ Cracks or blistering in walls, floors, or paint.
→ Damaged or weakened wooden furniture and structures.

TIPS FOR TERMITE CONTROL

✓ Inspection: Experts check property for termite activity and damage.
✓ Drilling: Small holes made at wall-floor junctions for chemical entry.
✓ Injection: Anti-termite chemical injected to form a protective barrier.
✓ Wood & Furniture Care: Spray/brush treatment with wood preservatives.
✓ Soil Treatment: Chemicals applied at foundation level for long-term protection.
✓ Sealing & Monitoring: Holes are neatly sealed, and regular inspections ensure protection with free re-treatment under warranty.

WHAT ARE BEDBUG ?

Do you notice small red bites at night, dark spots on your mattress/furniture, or rashes on your skin? These may be bedbugs. We provide professional bedbug treatment — thorough inspection, targeted spray/gel treatment, and repeat visits — so you can finally sleep in peace again.

Bedbugs are small, flat, oval, wingless insects that feed on human blood, often at night.They are reddish-brown and about the size of an apple seed, hiding in cracks of mattresses, bed frames, and furniture during the day.

SIGNS OF BEDBUG INFESTATION

→ Red bite marks in lines or clusters (mostly at night).
→ Black or brown spots on mattress, bed frame, sofa, or curtains.
→ Shed skins and eggs (tiny white grains).
→ In severe cases, a sweet, musty smell.
→ Live bedbugs hiding in mattress seams, furniture cracks, and crevices.
→ Itchy rashes or skin irritation after bites.
→ Stains from crushed bedbugs on bedding or furniture.

TIPS FOR BEDBUG CONTROL

✓ Inspection: Check mattresses, furniture, cracks, and hiding spots.
✓ 3-Step Plan: Three visits at 15-day intervals with spray and gel treatment.
✓ Customized Treatment: Localized or full-room treatment as needed.
✓ Mechanical Control: Vacuuming, steaming, and sealing cracks.
✓ Safe Insecticides: Low-toxic chemicals used only in affected areas.
✓ Protection & Prevention: Mattress covers, interceptor traps, regular follow-ups, and simple prevention tips like washing, luggage care, and decluttering ensure long-term safety.

BENEFITS OF BEDBUG CONTROL

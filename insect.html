WHAT ARE INSECT ?

Flies are not just annoying — they spread dangerous diseases like Typhoid, Cholera, Dysentery, and Food Poisoning. We provide professional fly control treatment using spray, baits & traps, ensuring hygiene and long-lasting relief.

Insects are small, invertebrate animals of the class Insecta, having three main body parts — head, thorax, and abdomen — with three pairs of jointed legs. Most insects also have antennae and usually one or two pairs of wings.

SIGNS OF INSECT INFESTATION

→ Large number of flies around kitchen, garbage, or drains.
→ Flies constantly sitting on food or surfaces.
→ Presence of eggs/larvae in waste or damp areas.
→ Unpleasant, persistent odor from decaying organic matter attracting flies.
→ Fly droppings or specks on walls, counters, and food surfaces.
→ Flies buzzing around pets, plants, or stagnant water.
→ Frequent sightings of maggots in garbage bins, drains, or compost areas
→ Contaminated food or surfaces leading to potential health risks
→ Increased activity during warmer months or in unhygienic areas.

TIPS FOR INSECT CONTROL

✓ Inspection: identify breeding sources & entry points.
✓ Residual Spray: applied to walls, windows & doors.
✓ Baits & Traps: fly traps placed strategically.
✓ Drain & Garbage Treatment: special chemicals used in waste areas.
✓ Preventive Guidance: cover garbage, use nets/screens, avoid stagnant water.

BENEFITS OF INSECT CONTROL

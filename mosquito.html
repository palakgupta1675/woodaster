WHAT ARE MOSQUITO ?

Mosquitoes don’t just cause itching — they spread dangerous diseases like Dengue, Malaria, and Chikungunya. We provide professional mosquito control treatment with a combination of spray + fogging methods, ensuring fast reduction and long-term relief.

Mosquitoes are small flying insects (family Culicidae) with slender bodies, long legs, two wings, and a long proboscis for piercing and sucking. Only the female bites animals or humans to get blood for egg development; many species are also vectors of diseases like malaria and dengue.

SIGNS OF MOSQUITO INFESTATION

→ Large number of mosquitoes visible at dusk/night.
→ Presence of larvae in water sources (tanks, plant pots, drains).
→ Red itchy marks on skin due to bites.
→ Buzzing sounds around sleeping or living areas, especially at night.
→ Mosquitoes resting on walls, ceilings, or dark corners during the day.
→ Stagnant water or damp areas attracting mosquito breeding.
→ Increased bites despite using nets or repellents, indicating high infestation levels.
→ Swelling or allergic reactions on skin from multiple mosquito bites

TIPS FOR MOSQUITO CONTROL

✓ Inspection: identify breeding sources & water collection points.
✓ Larvicidal Treatment: apply larva-killing chemicals in water sources.
✓ Fogging Treatment: outdoor fogging to kill flying mosquitoes.
✓ Residual Spray: indoor spray on walls, corners & resting areas.
✓ Preventive Guidance: avoid water stagnation, use nets/screens, hygiene tips.

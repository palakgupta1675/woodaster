WHAT ARE COCKROACH ?

Are cockroaches troubling your kitchen or bathroom? They spoil food, spread diseases, and make the environment unhygienic. We provide professional cockroach control treatment with gel + spray application, ensuring long-lasting results.

A cockroach is a flat, oval-bodied insect with a small head, long antennae, and six spiny legs. They are mostly nocturnal, fast movers, and hide in warm, dark places. Cockroaches have a tough exoskeleton and many species have wings, though they rarely fly long distances. They are highly adaptable and can survive in a wide range of environments.

SIGNS OF COCKROACH INFESTATION

→ Seeing cockroaches in kitchen, bathroom, or storage areas.
→ Small black fecal spots or egg cases (ootheca).
→ Strong musty odor in severe infestations.
→ Smear marks or streaks on walls and surfaces.
→ Shed skins or exoskeletons from molting.
→ Damaged or contaminated food and packaging.
→ Hiding in cracks, crevices, and behind appliances.

TIPS FOR COCKROACH CONTROL

✓ Inspection: identify infestation level & hiding spots.
✓ Gel Baiting: applied in kitchen drawers, cabinets, appliances.
✓ Spray Treatment: cracks, corners, drainage, under sink, fridge & stove.
✓ Low-toxic chemicals: safe for children & pets.
✓ Follow-up visit: re-inspection after 15–30 days if required.

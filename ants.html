WHAT ARE ANTS ?

Are ants troubling your home or kitchen? They spoil food, bite, and spread bacteria. We provide professional ant control treatment with gel + spray application that targets ants at the root for long-lasting relief.

Ants are small social insects (family Formicidae) with bodies divided into head, thorax, and abdomen joined by a narrow “waist”. Their antennae are elbowed, and their coloration ranges from black, brown, red, or yellow. They live in organized colonies with roles like workers, soldiers, and a queen.

SIGNS OF ANTS INFESTATION

→ Ant trails leading to food sources.
→ Cracks, kitchen areas, sugar or sweets attracting ants.
→ Seasonal spikes in large numbers.
→ Tiny mounds of soil or debris near entry points.
→ Ants nesting in walls, cabinets, or under floors.
→ Damaged plants or wooden structures in case of carpenter ants.
→ Presence of winged ants indoors, especially during mating season.
→ Sweet or sticky residues left behind, like honeydew from aphid-attending ants.

TIPS FOR ANTS CONTROL

→ Inspection: checking infestation level.
→ Gel Treatment: applied in kitchen & drawers.
→ Spray Treatment: cracks, corners & entry points.
→ Low-toxic chemicals: safe for children & pets.
→ Follow-up visit: re-inspection if needed.
